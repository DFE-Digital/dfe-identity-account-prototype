Congratulations on being awarded qualified teacher status (QTS)

The Teaching Regulation Agency (TRA) has awarded you qualified teacher status (QTS). The TRA is responsible for the regulation of the teaching profession in England and is sponsored by the Department for Education (DfE).

Get your QTS certificate

You can now download your QTS certificate. You’ll need to retain a copy of your certificate to present to employers as evidence of your teaching status.

Employers can contact the TRA independently to verify your teaching status.

Get your certificate In the Access your teaching qualifications service. You’ll need to create a {{ data.tsa }} to sign in.

www.access-your-teaching-qualifications.education.gov.uk

To sign in to Access your teaching qualifications, you’ll need to create a {{ data.tsa }}. It only takes a minute to create an account and once you have one, you can use it to sign in to different DfE teacher services.

You can also use it to update your details. To create an account to access your teaching qualifications, you’ll need your:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • mobile phone number

Certificates

The certificates are in an Adobe PDF format and are best viewed using up-to-date Adobe reader software. If you’re using a mobile device, you’ll need to get the free app.

www.adobe.com/uk/acrobat/pdf-reader.html

Search for a job in teaching

You can search for a job in teaching by using the government's official Teaching Vacancies website:

www.teaching-vacancies.service.gov.uk

It’s used by schools to list their teaching roles and you can sign up for tailored job alerts.
